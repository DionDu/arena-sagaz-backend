# Account Deletion - Arena Sagaz

**Last updated:** June 28, 2026 · **Version:** 1.0

This page explains how to delete your **Arena Sagaz** account and what happens to your data.

## How to delete your account

**In the app (recommended):**
1. Open **Arena Sagaz** and sign in to your account.
2. Go to **Account** (or Profile) → **Delete account**.
3. Confirm. The deletion is processed and you are signed out.

**By email (alternative):**
- Send a request to **sharparena@santiagodata.com** from the **email of your account**, with the subject **"Delete my account - Arena Sagaz"**. We may ask for confirmation to verify your identity.

## What is deleted

When you delete your account, we remove or **anonymize** your personal data, including:
- email, display name, and date of birth;
- links to login providers (Google, Apple);
- device notification tokens.

## What may be retained

- **Anonymized data** (which does not identify you) may be kept for game statistics and the integrity of past matches.
- Records we must keep due to a **legal obligation** are kept only for the required period and then deleted.

## Timeline

Deletion is generally **immediate** in the app. Email requests are processed within **30 days**. Copies in backups are removed in the normal purge cycle.

## Note about guest mode

If you use the App as a **guest** (no account), there is no account to delete: your progress data stays **on your device**. To remove it, simply **uninstall** the App or clear the app's data in your system settings.

## Contact

Questions about deletion: **sharparena@santiagodata.com**.
