# Privacy Policy — Arena Sagaz

**Last updated:** June 28, 2026 · **Version:** 1.0

This Privacy Policy explains how the **Arena Sagaz** app ("App") collects, uses,
shares, and protects your personal data. By using the App, you agree to the
practices described here.

## 1. Data controller

- **Responsible party:** Santiago Data, a brand operated by Fernando Santiago da
  Silva (individual) — required to identify the data controller.
- **Contact (DPO):** **contato@santiagodata.com**.

We are the **controller** of the data processed in the App, including under
Brazil's **LGPD** and, where applicable, the **GDPR**.

## 2. Data we collect

**a) Provided by you:**
- **Email** (when you create an email account or the provider shares it);
- **Display name** (chosen nickname);
- **Date of birth** (for age verification);
- **Login method** and your account identifier at the provider (Google, Apple).

**b) Collected automatically:**
- **Device identifier** and **notification (push) token**;
- **Platform** (Android/iOS) and **App version**;
- **Usage and gameplay telemetry** (game stats, progress, XP);
- **Aggregated analytics** (via Google Analytics for Firebase).

**c) Guest mode:** if you play as a **guest**, no account is required; your progress
stays **on your device** and is not tied to an account.

> We do **not** collect sensitive data (race, health, biometrics, etc.) or payment
> card data (the App has no purchases in this version).

## 3. How we use data (purposes)

- Create and maintain your account and authenticate login;
- Save and sync your progress, XP, streak, and achievements;
- Send **notifications** you authorize (news, reminders);
- Verify **age** and apply rules for minors;
- Improve the App (diagnostics, aggregated metrics, abuse prevention);
- Comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Legal bases

- **Performance of a contract** (providing the App and account);
- **Consent** (marketing notifications, ad tracking);
- **Legitimate interests** (security, fraud prevention, product improvement);
- **Compliance with legal obligations**.

You may **withdraw consent** at any time in the App settings or via the contact
email.

## 5. Who we share with

We do not sell your data. We share with **processors** that help us operate the App,
under contract and only as necessary:

- **Google / Firebase** (Authentication, Cloud Messaging, Analytics) — login,
  notifications, and metrics;
- **Login providers** (Google, Apple) — only to authenticate;
- **Server infrastructure** (API and database hosting);
- **Google AdMob** *(when ads are enabled, in a future version)* — for minors, ads
  are **non-personalized**.

These partners may process data **outside Brazil**; when that happens, we apply
safeguards consistent with the LGPD.

## 6. Notifications

Push notifications depend on your device authorization. You can **turn them off** at
any time in your system or App settings.

## 7. Retention and deletion

We keep your data while your account exists. You can **delete your account** at any
time (see the **[Account Deletion](exclusao-conta.md)** page). On deletion, we remove
or **anonymize** your personal data; some records may be kept anonymized for game
statistics or to meet legal obligations.

## 8. Your rights

You may request: **confirmation** and **access**, **correction**,
**anonymization/erasure**, **portability**, **information** about sharing, and
**review** of automated decisions. To exercise your rights, write to
**contato@santiagodata.com**. We respond within the legal deadlines.

## 9. Children and teenagers

The App is intended for people aged **13 and older**. Minors must have the consent
and supervision of a parent or guardian. We do not show personalized ads to minors.
If we learn we collected a child's data without proper authorization, we will delete
it.

## 10. Security

We apply technical and organizational measures (encryption in transit, access
control, environment segregation) to protect your data. No system is 100% secure,
but we work to reduce risks.

## 11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy. Significant changes will be communicated in the App or by
another appropriate means, with a new "last updated" date.

## 12. Contact

Questions or requests: **contato@santiagodata.com**.
